About

Biased Truth is a character created by YonKaGor and featured in Another Mistake.

Personality

Biased Truth is shown to be self-loathing and plagued with feelings of sadness, and described to be "scared and guilty" all the time.[1] They push others away and bottle up their own feelings for the fear of hurting anyone.

Appearance

Biased Truth has white fur and dark gray fur on the inside of their ears, wrists down and tail tip, with dark grey eyes. As for their outfit, Biased Truth wears a blue jacket and a silver, fish necklace, and grey pants.

Trivia

  • Biased Truth is YonKaGor's first character to be attached to one of their songs
  • Biased Truth's outfit is inspired by the outfit Yon wore when performing the song live a year before.[2]
